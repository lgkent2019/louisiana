Growing up in South Louisiana

I grew up about 30 miles north of the Gulf of Mexico.I enjoyed a variety of music and food. Seafood was a main staple growing up. Along with rice and gravy. Louisiana has a rich atmosphere of music, food and history. My grandmother spoke a mix of French and English. I have French, German, and Native American ancestry. Learning the different traditions and cultures of my ancestry shows me just how much Louisiana culture has combined these plus others such as Spanish and African cultures.

Some Facts about Louisiana

  • Baton Rouge is the capitol.
  • The climate is subtropical.
  • The State seal is a pelican and her nest surrounded by the state motto.
  • The State motto is Union, Justice, Confidence.
  • Louisiana has 64 parishes.
  • The alligator is the state reptile.
  • The Bald Cypress is the state tree.
  • The magnolia is the state flower.
